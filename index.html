Springfest 2024

Welcome to the official Springfest 2024 website!
Springfest is our school's largest, and everyone's favorite event of the year, made by students for students.
This year's theme is “Dare to Dream”, and we warmly encourage you all to do so, and to share this spirit during Springfest.
Explore this website to learn about SF24's activities and other fun things, and we hope to see you all on May 2nd and/or 3rd!

Big Bosses

Each year, 4 students and 3 teachers make up Springfest's “Core Team” as the so-called “Big Bosses”.

This year, Adrian Bernabeu (S7FRC), Caroline Gombalova (S7FRA), Aristidis Fragoulis (S7DEA), Josephine de Boer (S7NLA), and Big Boss Teachers Patty Roig, Chrysi Spanou and Jochen Wustenberghs have taken on the task of creating Springfest.

We can't wait to share what we've been working on for the past few months, and hope you have as much fun at Springfest as we had organizing it.
